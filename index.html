Why

Music has the unique power to capture attention and awaken a wide range of emotions, which can be incredibly appealing to many people. Whether through gentle melodies that soothe the soul, driving rhythms that encourage movement, or lyrics that resonate with your own experiences and feelings, music can offer an escape, a means of expression, or simply an enriching background to everyday life. Its diversity means that everyone can find something that speaks directly to them, whether in a specific genre or through the works of a particular artist. What's more, music can also serve as a bridge between people, sharing common experiences or celebrating cultures across the globe.
